The All India Panchayati Development Authority (AIPDA) is an body which focuses on the development and empowerment of Panchayati Raj institutions across India. Panchayati Raj refers to the system of local self-governance in rural areas. AIPDA works towards strengthening these institutions, promoting decentralization, and supporting local governance initiatives. Their efforts might include capacity building, providing resources, advocating for policy changes, and facilitating community participation in decision-making processes at the grassroots level.
